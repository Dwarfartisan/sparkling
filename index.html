Sparkling

A Clojure library for Apache Spark: fast, fully-features, and developer friendly

Get Started! » Read doc guides »

Fast

Standing on the shoulder of giants. Branching off from clj-spark and flambo, we introduced several changes to really keep things running. Speed of execution basically was the driver to have our own library.

Clojuric

Sparkling provides functions to keep your fingers clean, hiding all the Scala/Java artifacts in nice, clojuric abstractions without bloating the overall system.

Production ready

Sparkling is used in our productive environment and battle tested. Don't rely on just toy projects.
